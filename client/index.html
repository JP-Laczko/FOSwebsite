What Are We About?

FOS (Fill Our Shoes) Sports Academy’s mission is simple; give back to the community and train the next generation of athletes to not only “fill our shoes” when it’s their time to dawn the Wolfpack Blue, but far exceed the success of the ones who came before them. FOS Sports Academy offers a unique perspective that even the “professionals” can not offer - providing direct access for your child to get a personalized 1 on 1 lesson with their favorite West Morris athlete. During their private lesson, your athlete will be exposed to many drills that are used at the varsity level and ultimately to evaluate players, learn from their coaches own successes/failures as a Long Valley youth athlete, and so much more limited information only somebody who has been in their shoes can offer. Along with this, FOS Sports Academy is committed to reaching as many youth athletes as possible—a driving force behind our focus on affordability, providing lessons at roughly 50% less than the often outrageous cost of a lesson with a “professional”.

- FOS Founder/CEO, Michael Ferry
