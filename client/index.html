Our Services

A message from FOS Founder/CEO Michael Ferry

The FOS Story begins in June following his graduation from West Morris Central, when Michael decided to give some baseball lessons to youth players in Long Valley. Fast forward 8 months later to February 2025 of his freshman year at UGA, where he had the vision to evolve his baseball sidehustle into a business called FOS Baseball Academy. The inagural staff of FOS included only 2 coaches and has exponentially grown ever since. The success of FOS Baseball turned into yet another expansion - FOS Sports Academy - a replication of FOS Baseball that now covers numerous additional sports.
